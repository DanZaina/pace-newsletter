Lipid Clinic

We're excited to announce the launch of our lipid clinic. Led by our experienced lipid specialists, this clinic aims to support patients in managing abnormally high cholesterol levels and reducing the risk of heart disease.

Whether you're seeking guidance on medication management or lifestyle modifications, our (OHIP-Covered) lipid clinic is here to support you on your journey towards optimal heart health.

Smart Garment Holters

We now offer smart garment holter monitors. These (OHIP-covered) cutting-edge monitors offer a more convenient and comfortable alternative to traditional holter monitors, allowing for continuous heart rhythm monitoring without the need for cumbersome wires or electrodes.

With our smart garment holter monitors, you can go about your daily activities with ease while still receiving accurate and reliable heart rhythm data.
